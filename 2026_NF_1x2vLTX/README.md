2026 LTX 1x2v gyrokinetic paper
-------------------------------

Input files used to generate the results presented in the paper titled "One dimensional gyrokinetic study of a
low-recycling scrape-off layer" by A. Maan, et al.

These simulations used [version b57c81630771 of Gkeyll](https://github.com/ammarhakim/gkeyll/commit/b57c8163077123322c664f6bd06176e063f29c5d).

The simulations are organized into two folders:
- lowR: low recycling simulations.
- highR: high recycling simulations.

See the README.md in each folder for more detail.
